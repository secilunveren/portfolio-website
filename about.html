My Story

The plan was to work as a meteorological engineer after the university. But, if you are someone like me who likes questioning life then probably you know there is no one ideal path for you.

After graduating, everyone was surprised when I chose the planes as my office and to work with different colleagues every time. No, I wasn’t a pilot. I can’t stand being in that claustrophobic room. Although it was great at the beginning, later my body started to give me signals to leave that flight attendant job.

I was quite impressed when I first discovered UX & UI design fields. As a person who almost uses her two brain lobes equally, they resonated with me immediately. Discovering more about them and myself led me to enroll in a UI design bootcamp at CareerFoundry.

As a UI/UX designer, I find simple, straightforward, and intuitive designs natural. I enjoy using my creative & analytical sides at the same time, and talking about emotions. I prefer to be part of meaningful projects that aim to solve real life problems and of productive & supportive work environment. It is very satisfying to contribute to the world without ignoring my core values for a job.

When I'm not designing, I enjoy being on the beach, baking cakes, traveling, doing pilates, and experiencing new adventures.
